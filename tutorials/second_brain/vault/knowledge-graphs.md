# Knowledge graphs

## What they are

A knowledge graph represents information as entities (nodes) and relationships
(edges). Unlike a flat document store, the connections are first-class: you can
traverse them, rank them, and reason over them.

## Why they matter for notes

When your notes are a graph, a wikilink is a queryable edge. You can ask which
notes bridge two topics, or which are most central. This is the substrate that
[[neuro-symbolic]] systems build on.
