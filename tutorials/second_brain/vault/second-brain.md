# Second brain

## What it is

A second brain is an external, queryable memory: you capture ideas as notes and
let tooling surface them when relevant, so you do not have to remember
everything yourself. The value compounds as the notes interlink.

## How it is built

A practical second brain combines a capture method like [[zettelkasten]] with a
retrieval layer like [[graphrag]], so you can find notes by meaning and by the
links between them.
