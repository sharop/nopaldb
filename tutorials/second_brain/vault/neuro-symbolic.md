# Neuro-symbolic AI

## The idea

Neuro-symbolic AI combines neural methods (embeddings, pattern recognition) with
symbolic methods (graphs, logic, ontologies). The neural side handles fuzzy
similarity; the symbolic side handles structure and explainable reasoning.

## Where the two meet

A hybrid retrieval that fuses vector similarity with graph structure is a small
but concrete instance of this idea, grounded in [[knowledge-graphs]].
